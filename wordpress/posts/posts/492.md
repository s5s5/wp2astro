虽然昨天是七夕，没有佳人相伴，比较郁闷。不过今天早上一开信箱，挖哈哈哈哈哈～Flickr送我了一个Pro!!!

为什么要送我一个Pro呢，话说是N天前 [凯文](http://www.kevintony.com)（注意读时要绕舌）给我说Flickr变样子了，我马上拍马去看，刚到哪儿说时快那时快，只见屏幕一黑，Flcikr花花的就给我出了一张图图，上面还写着如果你把这个图打印出来，然后在上面发挥你的想象力画点什么，再用相机一照然后传到他那里去就有可能得到一个Pro账号～其实当时我也看不懂什么意思，就看到了“free”和“Pro”两个单词，本着米锐的直觉，我感觉有机会，然后就用Fireworks搞了一张图上去了，也没打印，因为想着可能得不了奖，所以搞的图还放上我的大大的LOGO，嘿嘿～  
![](http://static.flickr.com/52/193696225_54d9d9144e_d.jpg)  
[http://www.flickr.com/photos/s5s5/193696225/](http://www.flickr.com/photos/s5s5/193696225/)

然后呢，在我已经快要忘记这件事的时候，在今天早上收到了封信：

引用内容：

How about a glass of lemonade?

  We're absolutely stunned at the number of submissions to our impromptu colouring contest last week:  
[http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/flickrcolourcontest](http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/flickrcolourcontest)

With 1,100+ entries to date, we've decided to spread the love a little further than a single winner. (How could we select only one?)  
Because you rock, we're gifting you three months of Flickr pro as a thank you for your outstanding creativity during last week's downtime.

The Flickreenos

\*\* Please note that "reply to" won't work for this FlickrMail. If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to ask them in the Official Help Topic for Colouring Contest:  
[http://www.flickr.com/forums/help/25696/](http://www.flickr.com/forums/help/25696/)

还有更多的图大家可以去看 [http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/flickrcolourcontest](http://www.flickr.com/photos/tags/flickrcolourcontest)

于是乎，就这样中了奖了，呵呵～虽然还搞不懂Pro是什么意思，反正以后每个月可以上传2G的图到Flickr啦～
